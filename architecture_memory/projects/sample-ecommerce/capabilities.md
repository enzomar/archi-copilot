# Business Capabilities - Sample E-Commerce

## Customer Management

Manages customer accounts, profiles, preferences, and authentication. Core to personalization and loyalty programs.

- Legacy CRM System (being replaced)
- Customer Profile Service (new)
- Identity Provider (Auth0)

## Product Catalog

Maintains product information, categories, pricing, and inventory availability.

- Product Information Management (PIM)
- Inventory Service
- Pricing Engine

## Order Management

Handles order lifecycle from cart to fulfillment, including payment processing and order tracking.

- Shopping Cart Service
- Order Service
- Payment Gateway Integration
- Fulfillment Service

## Search & Discovery

Enables customers to find products through search, browse, and recommendations.

- Elasticsearch Cluster
- Recommendation Engine
- Search API

## Content Management

Manages marketing content, landing pages, and promotional campaigns.

- Headless CMS (Contentful)
- Campaign Management System
- A/B Testing Platform

## Analytics & Reporting

Provides business intelligence, customer analytics, and operational dashboards.

- Data Warehouse (Snowflake)
- Business Intelligence Tools
- Real-time Analytics Pipeline
